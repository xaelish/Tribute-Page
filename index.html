Marie Curie

The Woman Who Changed Science Forever

Here's a timeline of Marie Curie's life:

  • 1867 - Born in Warsaw, Poland.
  • 1891 - Moved to Paris to study at the Sorbonne.
  • 1898 - Discovered polonium and radium.
  • 1903 - Won the Nobel Prize in Physics.
  • 1911 - Won the Nobel Prize in Chemistry.
  • 1934 - Died in France.

"Nothing in life is to be feared, it is only to be understood. Now is the time to understand more, so that we may fear less."
